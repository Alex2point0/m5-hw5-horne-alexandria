My Favorite Artist: The Foo Fighters

About the Band

Melding heavy rock guitars, driving punk sensibilities, and pretty melodies, Foo Fighters rose to become one of the biggest acts to emerge out of the alt-rock boom of the 1990s. Led by singer Dave Grohl, the former powerhouse drummer for Nirvana, Foo Fighters initially debuted as a solo project in 1995.

Discography

Their discography consists of 10 studio albums, 10 extended plays (EPs), six video albums, and 57 singles.
